# KundliAI

**Authentic Vedic Kundli readings, generated from your birth details — right inside Claude.**

KundliAI gives Claude the depth of a 30-year Vedic astrologer. Share your birth date, time, and place, and Claude produces a deeply personal kundli covering your lagna, career, marriage, health, and 2025–26 predictions — written in clear English, with specific months, practical remedies, and personalised affirmations.

This plugin packages the same prompts used by KundliAI's professional kundli service into six focused skills you can invoke directly.

## Skills included

| Skill                          | What it does                                                                                  |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `kundli-basic-report`          | A 1,500-word starter report — lagna, career strengths, lucky periods 2025–26, and affirmation |
| `kundli-detailed-report`       | The full 2,500-word reading covering personality, career, marriage, health, and month-by-month forecast — answers one specific question |
| `kundli-premium-report`        | A 3,000-word premium reading bundled with Kundli Milan compatibility analysis for two charts  |
| `kundli-milan-compatibility`   | Standalone Ashtakoota / Guna Milan score and verdict for two birth charts                     |
| `monthly-horoscope`            | Personalised month-ahead forecast covering career, love, health, and the power week           |
| `daily-rashifal`               | A short daily forecast by moon sign for the current day                                       |

## How to use

After installing, just ask Claude things like:

- *"Generate my kundli — born 14 March 1995, 06:42 AM, Pune"*
- *"Give me a detailed kundli reading — DOB 23 August 1988, 10:15 PM, Chennai. My question: when will I get married?"*
- *"Run a Kundli Milan match between two charts"*
- *"What does May 2026 look like for a Scorpio moon?"*
- *"Today's rashifal for Mesha"*

Claude will trigger the right skill, ask for any missing birth details, and produce the report.

## What you'll need

For most readings: **name, date of birth, exact time of birth, and place of birth (city + state).**

The more accurate the time of birth, the more accurate the lagna and predictions. If you don't know the exact minute, give the closest you remember — Claude will still produce a useful chart.
